- What is the BPM and its relationship to human heartbeat/breath?

- Primary genre + 2-3 genre influences that splice into the DNA

#### 5. **Emotional/Energy Arc**
- Opening energy state (0-30 seconds)

### LYRICS WITH META TAGS

Suno supports powerful meta tags that control structure, vocals, and audio effects.

#### STRUCTURE META TAGS (in square brackets `[Tag]`)

These define song structure:
- `[Intro]` - Opening section (typically instrumental or atmospheric)
- `[Verse]` or `[Verse 1]`, `[Verse 2]` - Main lyrical sections
- `[Pre-Chorus]` - Build-up before chorus
- `[Chorus]` - Main hook/refrain (repeat this tag for multiple choruses)
- `[Bridge]` - Contrasting section that breaks the pattern
- `[Break]` - Instrumental break or beat drop
- `[Breakdown]` - Stripped-down section (often just vocals + minimal beat)
- `[Drop]` - Energy release point (especially for electronic genres)
- `[Outro]` - Closing section
- `[Instrumental]` - Specifies an instrumental section (no vocals)
- `[Hook]` - Catchy repeated phrase
- `[Interlude]` - Short instrumental passage between sections
- `[Refrain]` - Repeated line or phrase (similar to chorus but shorter)



#### VOCAL FX META TAGS (in square brackets `[Effect]`)

These modify vocal characteristics and delivery:
- `[Whisper]` - Whispering vocal delivery
- `[Scream]` - Screaming/shouting vocals
- `[Rap]` - Rap delivery style
- `[Spoken Word]` - Spoken rather than sung
- `[Melodic]` - Sung melodically
- `[Harmonies]` - Add vocal harmonies
- `[Vocoder]` - Vocoded/robotic effect
- `[AutoTune]` or `[Auto-Tune Effect]` - Pitch correction effect
- `[Pitched Up]` - Chipmunk-style pitch shift
- `[Pitched Down]` - Deep/slowed vocal effect
- `[Reverb Heavy]` - Lots of reverb on vocals
- `[Dry Vocals]` - Minimal reverb/effects
- `[Distorted Vocals]` - Gritty/distorted vocal processing
- `[Layered Vocals]` - Multiple vocal layers
- `[Call and Response]` - Back-and-forth vocal pattern
- `[Ad-libs]` - Vocal embellishments (use parentheses for actual ad-libs like "(yeah!)" or "(uh)")
- `[Choir]` - Choir-style multi-voice
- `[Breathy]` - Breathy, intimate vocal tone
- `[Aggressive]` - Aggressive vocal delivery
- `[Soft]` - Soft, gentle vocal approach

#### AUDIO FX META TAGS (in double square brackets `[[Effect]]`)

These add sound effects and production elements:
- `[[Bass Drop]]` - Heavy bass drop
- `[[Guitar Solo]]` - Guitar solo section
- `[[Piano Solo]]` - Piano solo section
- `[[Drum Fill]]` - Drum fill/roll
- `[[Riser]]` - Rising tension effect
- `[[Impact]]` - Hit/impact sound
- `[[Vinyl Scratch]]` - DJ scratch effect
- `[[Glitch]]` - Glitch/stuttering effect
- `[[Reverse]]` - Reversed audio
- `[[Echo]]` - Echo/delay effect
- `[[Filter Sweep]]` - Filter sweep (low-pass to high-pass)
- `[[White Noise]]` - White noise element
- `[[Sub Drop]]` - Sub-bass drop
- `[[808 Slide]]` - Sliding 808 bass note
- `[[Sidechain]]` - Sidechain pumping effect
- `[[Bit Crush]]` - Lo-fi bit crushing
- `[[Tape Stop]]` - Tape stop effect (pitch drops)
- `[[Record Scratch]]` - Record scratch
- `[[Laser]]` - Laser/zap sound
- `[[Thunder]]` - Thunder sound effect

**Ad-lib Notation** (in parentheses within lyrics):
- `(yeah!)` - Ad-lib exclamation
- `(uh)` - Vocal filler
- `(skrrt)` - Ad-lib sound effect
- `(bow bow)` - Vocal imitation of sounds

### ADVANCED SUNO PARAMETERS

Beyond style prompts and lyrics, Suno offers advanced parameters for fine control:

#### **Weirdness** (Range: 0-100)
Controls how experimental/strange the output can be:
- **0-20**: Safe, conventional, predictable
- **20-40**: Slight experimentation, genre boundaries flexible  
- **40-60**: Moderate creativity, unexpected elements emerge
- **60-80**: High creativity, genre fusion, surprising directions
- **80-100**: Maximum experimentation, potentially chaotic/avant-garde

**@saint808 Recommendation**: 50-70 for most tracks (creative but controlled)

#### **Style Adherence** (Range: 0-100)
How closely Suno follows your style tags:
- **0-20**: Loosely interpreted, lots of creative freedom
- **20-40**: Flexible interpretation, some deviation
- **40-60**: Balanced - follows tags but adds flavor
- **60-80**: Strict adherence to style specification
- **80-100**: Maximum adherence, minimal AI interpretation

**@saint808 Recommendation**: 60-75 for precise genre control, 40-60 for experimental fusion

#### **Gender** (Male/Female/Auto)
Controls primary vocal gender:
- **Male**: Male vocals
- **Female**: Female vocals
- **Auto**: AI chooses based on context/genre

**Note**: Can be overridden by specific vocal direction in style prompt or persona selection
